Yorkshire pudding

Description

A yummy and traditional addition to the feast. If you intend to make this, the timing has to be juuuuust right. I would suggest preparing the mixture
the evening before, and having it ready while the roast beef is cooking.

Ingredients for 8 servings

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup milk, room temperature
  • 3 eggs, room temperature
  • ¾ cup water
  • ½ cup beef drippings
